Requirements for admission



Admission requirements for Bachelor programmes are:

•An entrance examination comparable to a upper secondary school leaving certificate

•Proof of proficiency in English, if the programme is taught in English


Admission requirements for Candidatus/Master programmes are:

•an internationally recognised Bachelor's degree

•proof of proficiency in English, if the programme is taught in English


To embark on a PhD, you generally need to have a Master's/Candidatus degree or equivalent.
